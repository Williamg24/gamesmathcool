About Us

Games Math Cool is a complete knock off of Cool Math Games. Our website steals the same concept of having many easy to play games at your disposal. We have many many many single and multiplayer games which you can find on our home page. Games Math Cool put their blood sweat and tears into creating a website so that you can spend every waking second playing these "difficult" games. This website isn't just a website. It's symbolic of the unity, passion, and perseverance each of our users hold. We don't think of our users as just player 1, 2 or 3. We think of them as family and the sacrifice each of them made to play our games. Thank you and good night America!
